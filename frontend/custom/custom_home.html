CUSTOM

Custom mode is the mode where you decide on the rules !

You get to choose the mode, interval of seconds, maximum number up until which times tables will generate

and the amount of questions you can get wrong before the game ends


On the loading of the page, you willbe greeted with the starting screen where you can define the rules of the game.

When ready press the "Ready" button and off you go !

So head on over there and have fun !
