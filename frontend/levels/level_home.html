LEVELS

Welcome to the levels! An excellent way to get better at times tables whilst getting extreamly fustrated!

Each level will generate times tables up to twelve but at different intervals of time

    Eg:
  • Level 1 = 10 sec per question
  • Level 2 = 8 sec per question
  • etc...

You are allowed three wrong answers (strikes) before it stops

However math questions will just keep coming as long as you answer correctly so its up to you to decide when you're ready to move on

Have fun!
